剧社计划每年排演两部戏，具体招新时间为：

    组委会：2018 Lent (点此报名) 职位信息

    剧组成员：2018 Michaelmas

    演员：2018 Easter (2018 Michaemas剧目)；2018 Michaelmas (2019 Lent剧目)

剧社在这些时间之外不招纳新人。

剧社下属于CUCCS，只有CUCCS会员才能加入剧社。我们欢迎所有CUCCS会员报名，只要通过组委会的面试，你就能成为剧社的一员！


The CDC plans to produce two plays each year.

Recruitment takes place during

    CDC committees: Lent Term 2018 (Click here to apply)

    Crew: Michaelmas Term 2018

    Casts: 2018 Michaelmas Term Play: Easter Term 2018; 2019 Annual Play (Lent Term): Michaelmas Term 2018

CDC does not recruit new members during any other times of the year.

CDC is subsidiary to CUCCS. To become eligible for the club, one needs to obtain CUCCS membership. CDC opens to every member of CUCCS in principle, subject to passing one or two interviews conducted by CDC committee.
