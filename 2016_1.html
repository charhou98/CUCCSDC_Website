我们的荆轲 Our Assassin

Jan 2016

简介 Introduction

战国末年，群雄逐鹿，秦国一家独大，势吞天下。曾为秦国人质的燕太子丹仇恨惶恐之余，征寻刺客，谋划刺杀秦王嬴政.荆轲拜访剑客田光企图摆脱落拓的现状，不想田光却以性命相交将刺秦大业委托于他。荆轲尚在接受与推拒间徘徊时，燕太子丹又以豪宅、美姬相赠.期间，荆轲爱上了太子丹赠予的美人燕姬。心思婉转的燕姬与荆轲演习刺秦的经过，一语道破荆轲的困境，并且指出了一条晦暗得令人瞠目却又堂皇得青史留名的解决之道……这部作品中不仅将故事本身的内容进行了颠覆性诠释，甚至一贯为大家所认可的历史观也被进行了解构，而作者莫言却认为自己并没有去解构历史，“我只是把古人和现代人之间的障碍拆除了。”

The play recounts the legend of an assassin called Jing Ke from the Warring States period in a novel way, incorporating many modern elements (hence the name‘our Assassin’). Jing Ke, from the state of Yan in ancient China, is a talented assassin put on a secret mission of assassinating the Emperor of Qin due to the threats posed by Qin’s imperialistic intentions. Jing Ke was given this mission by Prince Dan of State Yan, who gifted him expensive food, a villa, and, most importantly, Yan-Ji (Lady of Yan), who served both the Emperor of Qin and Prince Dan himself. It was Yan-Ji who revealed to Jing Ke the dilemma he faced, which paved the way for Jing Ke to become one of the most renowned assassins in history. This play entitled a brand new explanation for the famous legend and broke the barrier between ancient people and modern people.
